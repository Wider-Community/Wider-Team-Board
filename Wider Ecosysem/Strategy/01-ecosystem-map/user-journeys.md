## Journey 1: The Student/Graduate

### Entry Persona
Fresh graduate or final-year student seeking to bridge the gap between education and employment.

### The Traditional Problem
```
TRADITIONAL PATH:
Graduate → Apply → Rejection ("No experience")
                         │
                         ▼
              Loop of frustration
              (Need experience to get hired,
               need hiring to get experience)
```

### The Wider Journey

#### Phase 1: Discovery (Wider Community)

**Trigger:** Hears about Wider from university, friend, or online

**Actions:**
- Creates Wider Community profile
- Explores philosophy and success stories
- Discovers the Merciful Welcome principle
- Understands multi-role possibility

**Mindset Shift:**
```
FROM: "I'm just a student with no experience"
TO:   "I'm high-quality metal ready to be forged"
```

#### Phase 2: The Forge Entry (Wider Interns)

**Trigger:** Applies to Wider Interns program

**Actions:**
- Submits application with authentic story
- Undergoes assessment (potential + commitment, not credentials)
- Gets matched to team and mentor
- Receives first objective

**What They Experience:**
- Real projects with real stakes
- Supervised contribution
- Structured learning alongside delivery
- First Shields earned

#### Phase 3: Forging (Wider Smith + Wider Grow)

**Trigger:** Active intern contributing to ecosystem

**Actions:**
- Moves through Reputation Engine stages:
  1. Theory → Study, certify
  2. Experimentation → Practice in sandbox
  3. Monetization → Use skill in real project
  4. Verification → Receive testimonials

**What They Experience:**
- Accumulating Shields (verified achievements)
- Points accumulation (reputation currency)
- Discovery of where their capabilities create most value
- Growth Loop: Discover → Embrace → Pivot

#### Phase 4: Emergence (Wider Network)

**Trigger:** Proven through forge, ready for certification

**Actions:**
- Completes Wider Process training
- Passes certification requirements
- Earns "Wider Prime" badge
- Enters Network as verified member

**What They Experience:**
- Priority routing to projects
- Direct project revenue (Credits)
- Continuing reputation compound
- Multiple role possibility (employee + entrepreneur)

#### Phase 5: Expansion (Multiple Paths)

**Branch A: Continued Network Member**
- Builds expertise and reputation
- Takes on bigger projects
- Mentors next generation

**Branch B: Entrepreneur (Wider Rise)**
- Graduation project becomes startup
- Accesses Wider Ventures capital (digital, human, network)
- Validates through ecosystem delivery
- Presents to Wider VCs with proven traction

**Branch C: Core Team**
- Joins Wider Team as employee
- Equity stake
- Governs the system that forged them

## Journey 2: The Experienced Talent/Freelancer

### Entry Persona
Professional with existing skills seeking better opportunities, community, and career acceleration.

### The Traditional Problem
```
TRADITIONAL PATH:
Freelance → Race to bottom on price
          → Constantly prove yourself
          → No reputation compound
          → Start from zero each platform
```

### The Wider Journey

#### Phase 1: Discovery (Wider Community)

**Trigger:** Frustrated with current freelance platforms or employment

**Actions:**
- Creates unified profile
- Imports existing work and testimonials
- Connects external content (Medium, LinkedIn, portfolio)
- Explores community philosophy

**Mindset Shift:**
```
FROM: "I have to hustle for every gig"
TO:   "The ecosystem will talk about me"
```

#### Phase 2: Validation (Wider Marketplace)

**Trigger:** Lists services or assets in marketplace

**Actions:**
- Creates detailed listings (Effort-Based Profiling)
- Receives AI-guided feedback on criteria
- Completes initial smaller engagements
- Builds Wider-native reputation

**What They Experience:**
- Native validation filters out low-quality competition
- Recommendation Engine surfaces relevant opportunities
- Work compounds into reputation

#### Phase 3: Network Certification (Wider Network)

**Trigger:** Meets criteria for Network membership

**Actions:**
- Completes Wider Process training
- Bridges gaps in SLAs, work ethics, quality standards
- Earns "Wider Prime" badge
- Accesses premium tier

**What They Experience:**
- Priority project routing
- Lower platform fees (Points influence Credits)
- Team formation opportunities
- Access to Hub enterprise projects

#### Phase 4: Force Multiplication

**Trigger:** Established in Network, seeking more

**Options:**

**A. Team Formation**
- Joins or leads specialized team (NLP, Data, Content, etc.)
- Combined capabilities attract larger projects
- "Your story completes their story"

**B. Asset Monetization**
- Creates reusable components
- Lists in Marketplace
- Generates passive income from past work

**C. Hub Integration**
- Company capability visible in Hub
- Enterprise project opportunities
- Partnership formation

**D. Rise Path**
- Own startup through Ventures
- Investment through VCs
- Leverage all ecosystem capital

## Journey 3: The Client/Company

### Entry Persona
Business needing talent, services, or digital solutions with frustration at traditional sourcing.

### The Traditional Problem
```
TRADITIONAL PATH:
Need → Recruiter fees (massive)
    → Freelance platforms (noise, quality risk)
    → Outsourcing firms (layers, cost inflation)
    → Internal hiring (months, uncertain outcome)
```

### The Wider Journey

#### Phase 1: Discovery (Client Portal)

**Trigger:** Searching for talent, solutions, or partnerships

**Actions:**
- Explores Client Community Portal
- Views active projects demonstration
- Discovers verified members
- Sees real-time ecosystem activity

**Mindset Shift:**
```
FROM: "Hiring is a leap of faith"
TO:   "I can see verified capability before commitment"
```

#### Phase 2: Exploration (Portal + Hub)

**Trigger:** Interest in specific capabilities

**Actions:**
- Filters ecosystem through specific lenses
- Reviews member profiles with verified history
- Explores ready-to-use products
- Connects with Hub for business integration

**What They Experience:**
- Proof of capability, not just claims
- Structured navigation to exact needs
- Transparent pricing models
- Validation-first methodology introduction

#### Phase 3: Small Validation (Marketplace or Network)

**Trigger:** Ready to test with limited commitment

**Actions:**
- Posts initial project or gig
- Receives matched proposals (not flood)
- Selects based on verified track record
- Experiences first milestone delivery

**What They Experience:**
- Recommendation Engine matching (not keyword spam)
- Wider Prime guaranteed quality
- Milestone-based payment (pay for delivery, not effort)
- Evidence before major commitment

#### Phase 4: Scaled Engagement (Hub Integration)

**Trigger:** Validated quality, ready for larger engagement

**Options:**

**A. Project-Based**
- Larger projects through Network teams
- Machine-assisted delivery (cost efficiency)
- Multi-milestone governance

**B. Strategic Partnership (Hub)**
- Deep integration with Hub
- Cost Aggregation: Share resources with other clients
- Resource Syndication: Access elite talent at shared cost
- Capability R&D: Projects that build ecosystem IP

**C. Digital Transformation**
- Consulting + implementation
- Labs AI models integration
- Ongoing machine access

#### Phase 5: Ecosystem Partnership

**Trigger:** Deep value realized, strategic opportunity

**Options:**

**A. Hub Partner**
- Become regional/industry hub
- Onboard local talent and clients
- Revenue share model

**B. Labs Partnership**
- Hardware investment (CSP model)
- AI software makes hardware profitable
- Long-term strategic alignment

**C. Rise Investment**
- Invest in ecosystem startups
- Access validated deal flow
- Participate in zero-waste investment environment

## Journey 4: The Entrepreneur/Startup Founder

### Entry Persona
Founder with an idea, possibly technical, seeking resources beyond just funding.

### The Traditional Problem
```
TRADITIONAL PATH:
Idea → Need funding → Pitch → Rejection (or dilution)
                         │
                         ▼
           Rebuild team, infrastructure, market
           access from scratch for each venture
```

### The Wider Journey

#### Phase 1: Discovery (Community + Labs)

**Trigger:** Seeks alternative to traditional VC path

**Actions:**
- Explores Wider Labs approach
- Understands three capitals (digital, human, network)
- Discovers Zero Waste philosophy
- Connects to community

**Mindset Shift:**
```
FROM: "I need money to build"
TO:   "I need to demonstrate value with ecosystem resources"
```

#### Phase 2: The Product Master's Journey Begins

**Stage: Battle of Uncertainty**
- Sees opportunities everywhere
- Fighting internal battle of clarity
- Wider embraces uncertainty, doesn't wipe it out

**Actions:**
- Engages with Wider Ventures
- Accesses initial digital capital (machines, assets)
- Connects to human capital (network talent)
- Begins small validation experiments

#### Phase 3: Validation Through Ecosystem

**Trigger:** First attempts, possibly first failures

**Stage: First Collapse → Resurrected Identity**
- Initial vision may fail
- Learns from ecosystem feedback
- Pivots with new wisdom
- Identity clarifies through the forge

**Actions:**
- Runs proofs-of-concept using Coding Machine
- Validates market fit within community
- Iterates based on real feedback
- Builds with minimal waste

**What They Experience:**
- Resources scale with commitment
- Community provides early customers
- Infrastructure already exists
- Knowledge recycled, not lost

#### Phase 4: Traction Proof (Ventures → VCs)

**Trigger:** Demonstrated value through ecosystem

**Stage: Inevitable Realization → Unstoppable Momentum**
- Vision aligns with reality
- Multi-dimensional strategy
- Momentum becomes unstoppable

**Actions:**
- Graduates from Ventures
- Presents to VCs with proven traction
- Shows efficient resource utilization
- Demonstrates market fit within community

**What They Experience:**
- Not pitching hypotheticals—showing proof
- Digital capital already deployed
- Team formed from network
- Customer validation from ecosystem

#### Phase 5: Scale with Ecosystem

**Options:**

**A. Wider-Integrated Startup**
- Continues using ecosystem infrastructure
- Draws talent from Network
- Distributes through Hub
- Becomes community member forever

**B. Labs Product Partner**
- Model trained once, deployed as revenue asset
- Partner covers operational costs
- Continuous revenue through ecosystem

**C. Tribes Model**
- Decentralized funding from family/relatives
- Wider prepares financial readiness
- Localized legal protection
- Value-based partnerships

## Journey 5: The University/Academic Partner

### Entry Persona
University seeking to bridge gap between education and industry, commercialize research, improve graduate outcomes.

### The Wider Journey

#### Phase 1: Hub Partner Discovery

**Trigger:** Seeking industry partnerships, graduate placement, research commercialization

**Actions:**
- Explores Hub Partner model
- Understands Academic Repurposing program
- Sees opportunity for innovation hub status

#### Phase 2: Partnership Formation

**Actions:**
- Signs Hub Partner agreement
- Trains faculty coordinators
- Identifies pilot student cohort
- Establishes community space (physical or virtual)

#### Phase 3: Academic Repurposing Activation

**Traditional Problem:**
```
Research → Paper → Conference → Archive → Forgotten
```

**Wider Solution:**
```
Research → Wider Labs → Product → Market → Revenue (with student ownership)
```

**Actions:**
- Faculty submit research for commercial assessment
- Students' graduation projects evaluated for market potential
- Wider provides technical teams for prototyping
- Small-scale market validation
- Commercial deployment with revenue sharing

#### Phase 4: Talent Pipeline Operation

**Actions:**
- Students enter Wider Interns program
- Progress through Smith and Grow systems
- Graduate with verified portfolios (Shields)
- Transition to Network or Rise paths

**What University Experiences:**
- Improved graduate outcomes
- Research commercialization revenue
- Innovation hub reputation
- Industry partnership flow

#### Phase 5: Regional Leadership

**Trigger:** Established hub with proven outcomes

**Actions:**
- Becomes recognized regional innovation center
- Mentors emerging hubs
- Shapes ecosystem evolution
- Attracts talent and partners to region

## Journey Principles

### 1. Single Identity Across Journeys
One profile. One reputation. One history. Everything compounds.

### 2. Multi-Role Fluidity
Be student, intern, employee, entrepreneur simultaneously. No artificial silos.

### 3. Merciful Welcome
Resources before demands. Failure appreciated as journey. Opportunity guaranteed.

### 4. Value First
Experience benefits before commitment. Pay for delivery, not promises.

### 5. Story-Driven Connection
Your story completes their story. Individual successes woven into ecosystem narratives.
