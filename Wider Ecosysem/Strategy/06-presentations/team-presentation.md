## Slide 11: Dual Currency System

| Credits (Hard) | Points (Soft) |
|---------------|---------------|
| Financial, auditable | Behavioral, reputation-based |
| Purchased or earned via high-value work | Awarded for mentoring, sharing, reviews |
| Pay fees, reserve compute, buy assets | Unlock governance, priority, grants |
| Platform liability until redeemed | Off-balance-sheet metric |

**Golden Rule:** Points never convert to Credits. Points influence Credit flow.

## Slide 13: Machine Economics

**Activity-Based Costing (ABC)**

Each machine = independent cost center with:
- Direct costs (labor, GPU, licenses)
- Allocated costs (supercomputer, DevOps, security)
- Activity metrics tied to value creation

**Internal Transfer Pricing:** Cost + 5-15% margin when machines serve each other.

*Prevents resource overuse. Forces strategic prioritization.*
