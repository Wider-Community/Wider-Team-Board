## ðŸ”¢ Essential Mathematics for AI

Mathematics forms the backbone of all AI and machine learning algorithms. Understanding these concepts will help you grasp how models learn, optimize, and make predictions.

### ðŸ“ Linear Algebra

Linear algebra is fundamental to understanding how data is represented and transformed in machine learning. It covers vectors, matrices, transformations, and eigenvalues.

#### What You'll Learn
- Vectors and vector spaces
- Matrix operations and transformations
- Eigenvalues and eigenvectors
- Basis and dimensionality
- Dot products and projections

#### Why It Matters
- Data is represented as vectors and matrices
- Neural network layers are matrix operations
- Dimensionality reduction (PCA) relies on eigenvectors
- Understanding transformations in deep learning

### ðŸ“ˆ Calculus

Calculus is essential for understanding how machine learning models learn through optimization. Gradient descent, the backbone of neural network training, relies heavily on calculus.

#### What You'll Learn
- Derivatives and gradients
- Chain rule and backpropagation
- Partial derivatives and multivariable calculus
- Optimization and finding minima/maxima
- Integrals and areas under curves

#### Why It Matters
- Gradient descent optimization
- Understanding loss function minimization
- Backpropagation in neural networks
- Probability density functions
